About Malapascua


Malapascua Island is a small island located in the Cebu, Philippines, known for its stunning beaches, crystal clear waters, and rich marine life. It is a popular destination for divers, as it is one of the few places in the world where you can reliably see thresher sharks.

The island offers a laid-back atmosphere, perfect for relaxing and enjoying nature. Visitors can also engage in various activities such as snorkeling, island hopping, and exploring local culture and cuisine.

Best Time to Visit Malapascua

Thresher shark sightings are present each day.

High season (December to April) means less chance of rain, but also more people and sometimes higher prices. Hottest time is from March – May.
