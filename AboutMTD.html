OUR STORY AND HISTORY

MTD Diveshop 


We are located on Malapascua Island Logon Daanbantayan Cebu, just off the north tip of Cebu in the Philippines, located near the western end of this picture-perfect white sand beach.

We like to keep things small so that your dive experience is as personalized as possible. You won’t be just one insignificant diver among many. We keep groups as small as we can, usually only 4 per group.

Why Choose Us?

FUN TIME

At MTD Diveshop, our valued customers are our top priority. We strive to ensure that your vacation in Malapascua is nothing short of spectacular! We're passionate about diving and eager to showcase the best of what we offer. Our team, from owners to boat staff, shares this passion, creating a vibrant and enjoyable atmosphere. With laughter and camaraderie, we make every dive excursion a fun-filled experience.

EXPERIENCE MORE

Dive into the extraordinary with our experienced team. With over 15 years of diving in Malapascua, we bring unparalleled knowledge of the dive sites. Our expert guides ensure you make the most of every dive, uncovering hidden wonders and unforgettable moments beneath the waves. There are, quite simply, no dive shop owners or managers on Malapascua that have spent as much time on the island as our team at MTD Diveshop.

DIVING VENTURE

Dive effortlessly with MTD Diveshop. Upon arrival, simply unpack your gear, and we'll take care of the rest. From setting up equipment to assisting with scuba units, our dedicated staff ensures a seamless experience. After your dive, leave the heavy lifting to us—we'll transport and rinse your gear, allowing you to relax and enjoy your holiday to the fullest. We will also rinse your kit at the end of the day if you wish.
